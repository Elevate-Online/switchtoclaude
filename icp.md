## Who you are writing for on this property

**ICP 3 — ChatGPT Switcher.** Top-of-funnel individual who is either values-driven (OpenAI concerns) or practical (ChatGPT does not work for their use case anymore) and wants a free guide + two example prompts to feel like they can switch without losing their workflow.

1. **Values:** OpenAI Pentagon deal, Sam Altman drama, Microsoft entanglement
2. **Quality:** Claude writes better in their voice
3. **Privacy:** concerned about training data

The buying journey is NOT direct revenue on this property — it is INTO claudeforeveryone.com afterward. switchtoclaude.com is a lead magnet. Direct revenue ~$0. Indirect via email capture + nurture.
